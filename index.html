Forever Seen

When my soul chose you, it chose forever,

Like swans who bind one heart to one heart.

A vow unbroken. A love eternal.

A fate written before time began.

If the world changes and the stars fall silent,

You remain my constant, my one truth.

For like the swan that knows only one love,

My heart knows only you.

Not a choice. Not a chance. Not a maybe

But destiny.
